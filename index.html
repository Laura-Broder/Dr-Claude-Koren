About Our Service

We provide a wide range Treatment of malignant tumors of the female reproductive system: ovarian cancer, uterine body, cervix, and vaginal and vaginal cancer under the direction of Dr. Claude Koren.

In our medical center you can find all the various units necessary for the optimal treatment of tumors in the female reproductive system.

Treatment success is based on a multidisciplinary approach at all stages of patient care.

A decision on the type of treatment is made after an in-depth discussion by a multidisciplinary team that includes an oncologist, gynecologist, surgeon, pathologist and radiologist.

The combination of surgical techniques, chemotherapy and radiation therapies allows for a wide range of treatment options

Radiotherapy

We have a wide range of radiation treatments adapted to different types of tumors.
The equipment is innovative and includes:

Simulation - for computerized radiation field planning using a three-dimensional method D3 treatment planning, IMRT (Intensity Modulated Radiation Therapy).

5 linear accelerators - for providing three-dimensional radiation treatment, which allows for an optimal dose for growth while maintaining maximum healthy tissues.

A computerized treatment planning system.

Computerized quality control system.

Brachytherapy - In this technique, radioactive sources are inserted through the vagina. The treatment is given on an ambulatory basis at a high dose rate - HDR (High Dose Rate) and lasts up to about an hour.

Staff

The patient is closely accompanied by a multidisciplinary team of therapists that includes physicians, nurses, radiation technicians, and physicists.
